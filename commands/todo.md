# Open Todos

Find and surface all open, incomplete, or pending items across your knowledge directories.

Look for:
- Unchecked checkboxes (`- [ ]`)
- Items marked as pending, outstanding, or in-progress
- Anything with an overdue date (compare against today's date — run `date +%Y-%m-%d`)
- Items explicitly flagged as urgent or high priority

**Present findings as a single consolidated list**, grouped by knowledge directory or project. For each item include: what it is, where it came from, and any date it's associated with.

Highlight at the top:
- Overdue items
- Items due today or this week

If nothing is found, say so clearly. Do not invent tasks.

---
*Customise this command for your setup by editing `~/.claude/commands/todo.md`*
